# Resume Bullets

## Data Analyst / Product Analyst Bullets

- Built an end-to-end support intelligence analytics project using Python, SQLite, pandas, SQL, and Streamlit to analyze `7,148` synthetic SaaS support tickets across `320` accounts and `15` months of history.
- Designed a multi-table SaaS dataset linking support demand to product usage, feature requests, experiments, ARR bands, and renewal-risk signals to answer product and operations questions with dashboard-ready outputs.
- Developed SQL and Python analyses for ticket trends, CSAT, escalation rate, churn-risk segments, ARR exposure, and experiment performance; identified that performance issues had the worst CSAT (`2.48`) and enterprise accounts had the highest churn-risk ticket rate (`18.2%`).
- Built an explainable prioritization framework combining ticket frequency, severity, CSAT drag, ARR exposure, churn risk, and feature-request pressure to produce roadmap-ready issue rankings and business recommendations.

## AI Product / AI PM Bullets

- Designed a local, explainable AI workflow for support intelligence that classifies tickets, summarizes recurring themes, and prioritizes product pain points without relying on chatbot UX or heavy infrastructure.
- Evaluated a support ticket classifier on a noisy, account-held-out test split and achieved `0.938` accuracy and `0.925` macro-F1, then documented limitations, review rubrics, and failure cases for responsible deployment.
- Translated support operations data into product decisions by connecting AI-generated issue themes to ARR exposure, churn-risk indicators, and feature-request demand, including roadmap signals for dashboard reliability, billing clarity, and enterprise permissions.
- Authored PRD, personas, user stories, tracking plan, analytics spec, experiment ideas, and a launch recommendation memo to show end-to-end AI product thinking from workflow definition through measurement and rollout planning.

## 60-Second Interview Explanation

I built a portfolio project around a realistic SaaS problem instead of another chatbot demo. The project simulates a B2B SaaS company with support tickets, message threads, product usage, feature requests, experiments, and monthly account metrics. I used Python, SQL, pandas, and a lightweight local AI layer to classify tickets, summarize recurring pain points, and rank product issues based on volume, severity, CSAT impact, ARR exposure, and churn risk. Then I turned the outputs into a Streamlit dashboard, SQL reports, evaluation artifacts, and product docs. The project is strong for analytics roles because it shows metric design, segmentation, and business recommendations, and it is strong for AI product roles because it treats AI as a measurable workflow feature with evaluation and launch planning rather than a chatbot wrapper.
