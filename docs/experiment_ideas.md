# Experiment Ideas

## 1. Guided Onboarding Checklist

**Hypothesis**
In-product setup guidance will reduce onboarding and access tickets for new admin users.

**Primary metrics**
- onboarding ticket volume
- access ticket volume
- average resolution time
- CSAT for onboarding/access tickets

## 2. AI Queue Triage

**Hypothesis**
AI-assisted routing will reduce escalations and improve handling time for bug, performance, and integration tickets.

**Primary metrics**
- average resolution time
- escalation rate
- first-touch routing accuracy

## 3. Billing Clarity Redesign

**Hypothesis**
Clearer invoice line items and a self-serve billing explainer will reduce SMB billing confusion and refund pressure.

**Primary metrics**
- billing ticket volume
- refund flag rate
- billing CSAT

## 4. Enterprise Access Templates

**Hypothesis**
Prebuilt SSO and permission templates will reduce enterprise access friction and speed up rollout.

**Primary metrics**
- access ticket rate in enterprise
- time to resolution
- new-admin activation rate

## 5. Reliability Status Messaging

**Hypothesis**
Proactive in-app status messaging during performance incidents will reduce duplicate tickets and lower escalation pressure.

**Primary metrics**
- performance ticket volume
- duplicate ticket rate
- escalation rate
