# User Personas

## 1. Support Operations Manager

**Primary goal**
Reduce manual triage effort and identify where support workflows are breaking down.

**Current pain**
- weekly ticket reviews require manual tagging and screenshot-heavy summaries
- escalations feel reactive instead of systematic
- support leadership lacks a reliable view of recurring pain by segment

**Needs from this product**
- auto-classified tickets
- recurring-theme summaries by week and segment
- visibility into escalation rate, resolution time, and CSAT by issue type
- confidence that AI suggestions are explainable

**Key decisions**
- where to assign support coverage
- which workflows to automate
- which issues to escalate to product

## 2. Product Manager

**Primary goal**
Turn noisy support demand into a prioritization input for roadmap planning.

**Current pain**
- feature requests and support complaints are disconnected
- severe issues in high-ARR accounts can be hidden by higher-volume SMB noise
- roadmap debates lack a consistent prioritization framework

**Needs from this product**
- issue ranking based on frequency, severity, CSAT impact, ARR exposure, and churn risk
- segment-specific problem concentration
- experiment comparisons tied to support outcomes
- concise, reusable recommendation summaries

**Key decisions**
- which problem area to prioritize next
- which customer segment needs targeted product investment
- whether an operational fix or product fix should come first

## 3. Customer Success Lead

**Primary goal**
Protect renewals and expansions by identifying accounts or segments under pressure.

**Current pain**
- renewal-risk conversations happen too late
- support pain is visible anecdotally but not systematically
- feature demand from strategic accounts is hard to quantify

**Needs from this product**
- churn-risk ticket rate by segment
- high-ARR issue exposure
- visibility into experiment performance for onboarding and support interventions
- clear account-health implications from support patterns

**Key decisions**
- which accounts need proactive outreach
- where to focus enablement or onboarding interventions
- when to escalate product pain to leadership
